About the Author

Christine Grän was born in Graz, lived in Berlin, Bonn, Botswana and Hong Kong and is now at home in Munich. With her heroine Anna Marx she created one of the first female detectives in German crime fiction, and the series was filmed as a twelve-part television series. Grän was awarded the Marlowe and the Ernst Hoferichter Prize.

Why "Kalopsya Books"?

Kalopsya (From Ancient Greek καλοψία): The delusion of things being more beautiful than they are.
For me, the fascination in writing crime novels is to uncover the ugly and the dark below the surface of human existance in which things are never what they initially seem to be.
